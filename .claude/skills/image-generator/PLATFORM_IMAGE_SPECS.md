## Format-Priorisierung nach Content-Typ

### Blog-Artikel
1. Featured Image (1200x628)
2. LinkedIn Post (1200x627) - gleich verwendbar
3. Facebook Post (1200x630) - gleich verwendbar
4. Pinterest Pin (1000x1500)

### Social Media Post
1. Instagram Square (1080x1080)
2. LinkedIn Carousel (1080x1080)
3. Instagram Story (1080x1920)
4. Twitter (1200x675)

### Paid Ads
1. Meta: 1080x1080 + 1080x1920
2. Google: 300x250 + 728x90 + 320x100
3. LinkedIn: 1200x627

### Video Content
1. TikTok/Reels/Shorts: 1080x1920
2. YouTube Thumbnail: 1280x720
3. Feed Video: 1080x1080
